Top three activities to do in Tamil Nadu

Explore ancient temples

Visit architectural marvels like the Brihadeeswarar Temple and experience the spiritual and historical essence of Tamil Nadu.

Relax in the hills of Ooty

Enjoy a peaceful boat ride on Ooty Lake or walk through tea plantations in the Nilgiri hills for a refreshing experience.

Watch a Bharatanatyam performance

Witness Tamil Nadu’s classical dance form performed in traditional temples or cultural venues—an unforgettable visual treat.
